About the Group

The Sustainable Design Group is a research collective at the Department of Architecture, University of Cambridge. We explore the intersection of design practice, environmental performance, and social equity — working with communities across the globe.

Each marker on this map represents an active or completed research project. Click any marker to learn more about the work, the team involved, and links to publications.
